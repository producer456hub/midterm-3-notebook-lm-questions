# Midterm 3 — NotebookLM Practice Test

A browser-based, self-grading study tool with **two exams** to choose from:

1. **Standard Midterm** — 150 multiple-choice questions (6 options each) covering **Bone Tissue & the Skeletal System, Joints, and the Muscular System** (Weeks 7–11).
2. **Functional Exam** — 100 applied-physiology questions (5 options each) on *how* and *why* things work: homeostasis, chemistry, cellular transport, tissues, the integumentary system, and the functional physiology of bones, joints, muscles, and their neural control.

**Take it here:** https://producer456hub.github.io/midterm-3-notebook-lm-questions/

## How it works
- Pick an **exam**, then a **topic** (or the whole bank), and start.
- Answer the questions, then hit **Submit & grade**.
- Anything you miss drops into a **retry round** — you keep retaking only the missed questions, round after round, until you've gotten **every question correct**.
- Options: shuffle question order, shuffle answer order, and optional instant feedback.

Everything runs locally in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

## Files
- `index.html` — the quiz engine and styling
- `questions.js` — the 150-question Standard Midterm bank with answer keys
- `functional.js` — the 100-question Functional Exam bank with answer keys

**Standard Midterm:** 60 Bones (Q1–60) · 30 Joints (Q61–90) · 60 Muscles (Q91–150).
**Functional Exam:** topic-tagged across Homeostasis & Chemistry, Cells & Transport, Tissues, Integumentary, Bones, Joints, Muscles, and Nervous System.
