# Midterm 3 — NotebookLM Practice Test

A browser-based, self-grading practice test covering **Bone Tissue & the Skeletal System, Joints, and the Muscular System** (Weeks 7–11). 150 multiple-choice questions (6 options each).

**Take it here:** https://producer456hub.github.io/midterm-3-notebook-lm-questions/

## How it works
- Pick a topic (All 150, Bones, Joints, or Muscles) and start.
- Answer the questions, then hit **Submit & grade**.
- Anything you miss drops into a **retry round** — you keep retaking only the missed questions, round after round, until you've gotten **every question correct**.
- Options: shuffle question order, shuffle answer order, and optional instant feedback.

Everything runs locally in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

## Files
- `index.html` — the quiz engine and styling
- `questions.js` — the 150-question bank with answer keys

Question bank: 60 Bones (Q1–60) · 30 Joints (Q61–90) · 60 Muscles (Q91–150).
